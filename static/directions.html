Directions

View the directions for every trip leg that is included in the packets. The directions for each leg are identical across packets. For instance, the directions from Hanover to the Lodge that you see here are identical across packets.

It is very important that you double-check these routes. Google maps is used to generate these routes, and the service picks the top recommended route. You may or may not have a preference on which route the bus takes.
